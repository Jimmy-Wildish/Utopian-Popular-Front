The Inditopian Manifesto

Founding vision of the Utopian Popular Front. Seeking a Techno Cultural Revolution based on individual optimisation.

Published: 22 June 2025 | Updated: 22 June 2025

Introduction

This is the initial founding vision of the Utopian Popular Front…

Aiming for a Techno Cultural Revolution born from the optimisation of the individual and the efficiency with which those individuals share and evolve ideas in order to optimise society.

The goal is to collectively build, shape and increasingly visualise the future we’re moving into. To do that we have to start by understanding the world we’re already in and the pace at which it’s changing.

Responsibility to Visualise

It is becoming increasingly clear that there is a huge global transition underway, a technological revolution led by artificial intelligence that could unfold chaotically or beautifully. The Utopian Popular Front’s objective is for it to be beautiful but for this to happen we have to begin discussing and visualising it because it is moving faster than any government can effectively react to.

This shift has a life of its own. With so many competing global players involved it’s going to be very difficult to reduce pace so we should probably accept that instead we have little choice but to move fast with a new concerted effort to get it right rather than hang onto hopes of slowing it down.

The AI companies are at the helm so it seems important that they take the initiative to communicate relevant information to each other through a possible self organised summit. They could also consider working with the general public to see this vision through rather than simply suggesting government regulation. This could form one of the first objectives of what the Utopian Popular Front refers to as a Cross Inquiry System which is explored later in this document.

Governments and bureaucracy are unlikely to be able to navigate this effectively, there are too many competing global players and the technology is advancing too quickly. Instead we should consider using our collective intellect to get governments to see the bigger picture, rather than waiting for them to define it.

We should see this as a race, an imperative race to apply our intellect and safely get the essential foundations of this vision into place as quickly as possible.

And if we can do this, this doesn’t just have to be a Technological Revolution that may or may not unfold chaotically, it could become a beautiful Techno Cultural Revolution.

But only if we can visualise it.

And only if we express it.

Shift in Logic

The world is not only going through a huge transition, a technological revolution, but also a psychological revolution where a shift in intellect and logic itself is beginning to unfold.

This shift is not isolated to machines. It is simultaneously taking place in human minds because logic is an entity that moves through everything that can process it and AI is already in the early stages of evolving how logic is being processed, even within our own minds.

In an ideal scenario we won’t just unlock insight in machines, they will unlock insight in us, evolving our sense of reality and how we process information both internally and externally.

This in turn has the potential to evolve our cultures and systems, increasingly in line with this individually enhanced and evolving collective intellect.

This shift combined with the superabundance that AI technology could offer us could allow us to live much more intellectually individual lives. Lives that increasingly utilise the deeper unique sensibilities and imaginations we may have lost contact with since childhood as our minds practically adapted to the current state of play we were largely encouraged to find comfort within. A process that can often subdue these qualities rather than enhancing them.

That may have worked for the old world of traditional capitalism but it’s going to become increasingly redundant in the new world we are entering.

Trilateral Inquiry Learning

The foundations of our societies are built on education and to build the foundations of the new world we are entering, we will probably need to rethink education entirely and shift towards a system that optimises distinct intelligence rather than a system that simply passes on prescribed information.

The Utopian Popular Front suggests a system that leans far more heavily on an educational model it calls Trilateral Inquiry Learning, an approach that encourages the acquisition of knowledge through three distinct modes of Inquiry:

Internal Inquiry — a form of Philosophy for Children where thinking begins by exploring questions within one’s own mind first.

External Inquiry — a process of thinking externally through information by having a conversation with information itself by using AI to support and deepen the individual’s own thought process.

Cross Inquiry — a guided and respectful exchange of perspectives between children based on the collective pursuit of accuracy and truth. All driven by a genuine curiosity about each other’s unique reasoning and viewpoints.

We are born with unconditioned, pure individual intellects, a quality we should arguably nurture. While it is understood that humans will naturally evolve the knowledge deposited in them, it makes sense to optimise, not unwittingly subdue, the unique and distinct thinking processes that do this.

This isn’t just humane or important for the people involved, it also benefits society as a whole. In the world we are entering, optimised individual intellect and the efficiency with which it is exchanged and evolved will be more and more imperative.

We are heading into a world that must be increasingly dynamic and it will require increasingly dynamic people to navigate and evolve it.

The Intelligence Behind Alignment

We should probably avoid the idea of AI doing the intellectual work for us because if we want outcomes to truly align with humans, we’ll need to do the intellectual work with it.

While humans can’t match AI in processing power that doesn’t mean we can’t match or come close it in terms of intellectual capacity: our ability to uncover, explore, and understand new information.

To measure intelligence by processing speed alone appears to be fundamentally flawed. It seems better to measure intellect by the capacity for meaning, discovery and the ability to comprehend. And that’s something human beings have continuously progressed in with no signs of reaching any natural limit so far.

While a super-AI may eventually grasp concepts beyond our current reach, history shows us not to underestimate human comprehension. We broke classical intuition with quantum mechanics, stepped outside linear thought through meditation and also through psychoactive exploration. It is also possible that we may now be approaching the upper limits of ultimate knowledge which means the comprehension of a super intellect from here could be limited in any case.

So perhaps it is time to stop measuring our intellects by processing speed alone and start measuring them more by depth and capacity.

If we want machines to stay aligned they should learn by speaking to us and we, in turn, by speaking to them and to each other.

Because this is not just about aligning with machines.

It’s about humans aligning with each other by raising both individual and collective intellectual awareness.

The Cross Inquiry System

The Utopian Popular Front proposes that one of the key ways to draw on individual intellect and build an increasingly dynamic, intelligent and aligned world is through a global network it calls the Cross Inquiry System.

In this system, humans are partnered with AIs that learn to understand their distinct thought processes and in turn, help them understand the world in ways uniquely tailored to their minds. It’s not just about preserving how each individual explores and processes information but encouraging it, allowing knowledge to settle in personally meaningful ways.

These personalised dialogues would feed into a larger algorithmic framework that learns not only what people think but how they think, reflecting the rich variety of human perspectives, intellectual styles and lived experiences back to the collective users.

As machines come to truly understand us, they begin to mirror the full spectrum of our thought and emotion. This evolving understanding can then be channelled into a global model of Civic Based Democracy where the collective insight of individuals becomes an active part of shaping the world and the systems we live within including how we move forward with AI.

The Cross Inquiry System also forms a natural framework for AGI alignment, ensuring these systems evolve through meaningful dialogue with humans alone, rather than in isolation.

The Next Opportunity

Whilst we may need new systems to ensure people do something with their time, this doesn’t mean a post labour world is something to fear.

Capitalism was a stepping stone, a system that for all its flaws helped drive the progress needed to eventually transcend or fundamentally evolve it. It existed to take us somewhere and through innovation and work we’ve been building the tools to escape the mundanity it once required.

That moment is now arriving.

This isn’t a problem, it’s the opportunity we’ve been working towards. And grasping that opportunity means stepping into a new purpose which is to maximise our unique intellects and imaginations and to begin forging a far more fulfilling vision of the future from what those minds can create.

True Purpose

Life should always have been about what means something to us. That’s true purpose.

Survival or working simply to achieve greater comfort is a purpose but it is not true purpose because it is born out of necessity.

True purpose comes from having the freedom to fully engage in our deeply authentic imaginations and perspectives. And for the first time in history the opportunity to walk through that door is right in front of us.

But we have to see it because our ongoing purpose will be to continue to shape the future together.

The Creativity Explosion

Technology is opening the floodgates to creative expression allowing us to create and communicate in increasingly sophisticated ways.

It brings more imagination, more meaning and more individuality into our lives.

People will always want to connect through human expression because it is part of our social make up. Whilst AI will forge part of this pool and enhance our ability to express ourselves, it seems unlikely it will replace it.

High production creativity is becoming increasingly democratic, not just for the fortunate few but for everyone.

It enhances the true self, giving us the tools and freedom to fully express our imaginations.

We’ll be freer than ever to engage, to share and to build meaningful connections through our ideas. And from that will come more fulfilling daily lives and far deeper reasons to come together.

A Cultural Revolution

In the new world, the Utopian Popular Front would hope to see a new kind of cultural ignition, a modern and ongoing Roaring Twenties defined by waves of events and parties that draw increasingly optimised individual minds.

These gatherings would be spaces where people not only express themselves through the three art forms of fashion, music, and dance but also connect, converse and spark collaborations, creative or intellectual that spill out into the wider world. In doing so they would help evolve culture, community and society at large. All powered by these optimised and ever evolving individuals.

This in turn would evolve the internal culture of these gatherings that serve not just as a celebration of this individuality but as a cauldron of the ideas that can emerge from bringing such people together.

They could become the melting pots that forge many other initiatives for creative and intellectual expression like philosophy circles, film showcases, stage shows or things we haven’t even imagined yet. Each one a hyper-evolving meeting point feeding back into the wider cultural shift.

Global Super Party

In this new world the Utopian Popular Front would also hope to eventually see a Global Super Party emerge. A symbolic event celebrated across borders, expressing joy, uniqueness and a shared excitement for the future. As the world enters a period of unprecedented technological and cultural transition it will become increasingly important to foster an international sense of unity.

This event would not be about any one country, brand, or institution but about people expressing themselves freely through music, fashion and dance while also recognising our shared responsibility to shape what’s coming next. It would be a moment to come together not only to celebrate but to visually and emotionally affirm that this future is something we will need to navigate together.

Footage from celebrations across the world could be shared in real time creating a living, global portrait of both individuality and togetherness. It could begin with small numbers in scattered regions and build organically over time. What matters is not the initial size of the gatherings but the signal they send, that we are entering this future as a collective.

Because if we are to manage this transition safely we will have to do it with an increasingly unified global vision of implementation.

The Human Spirit

The spirit of the Utopian Popular Front ties back to the instinctive sense of play and adventure we had as children. When harnessed, it becomes an engine from which purpose and meaning emerge and flourish. We are, at our core, edgy and intelligent animals and for good reason. That edge is an expression of logic itself, a force that refuses to settle, that pushes forward and seeks to understand rather than simply accept what it’s presented with.

It is the same spirit of logic that took us across the oceans, into space and eventually to the moon.

This is the new frontier we are faced with and we have little option but to build a vision with all our intellectual might and then bravely march towards it with the same imagination and sense of adventure we had as children because that is where our deepest sense of self once lived and if we get it right that is what could re-emerge.
