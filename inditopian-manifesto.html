Published 22 June 2025  |  Updated 22 June 2025

Intro

This is the initial founding vision of the Utopian Popular Front… Aiming for a Techno-Cultural Revolution born from the optimisation of the individual and the efficiency with which those individuals share and evolve ideas in order to optimise society.

The goal is to collectively build, shape, and increasingly visualise the future we’re moving into. To do that we have to start by understanding the world we’re already in and the pace at which it’s changing.

Responsibility to Visualise

It is becoming increasingly clear that there is a huge global transition underway, a technological revolution led by artificial intelligence that could unfold chaotically or beautifully. The UPF’s objective is for it to be beautiful, but for this to happen we have to begin discussing and visualising it because it is moving faster than any government can effectively react to.
